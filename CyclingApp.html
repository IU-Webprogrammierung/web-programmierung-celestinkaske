Innovative bicycle monitor app

I am devoted to humanity-centered design, ensuring that my professional work revolves around creating digital experiences that prioritize empathy, inclusively, and meaningful connections with users.

Timeline

24.08.2023 - 24.11.2023

Skills

App design / Mobile / Wireframing / Protoyping / User Research / Applied UX / UX Testing

1.1. Competitor Analysis

The initial phase involved conducting thorough research and constructing a competitor analysis to gain insights into the existing landscape and identify potential app sectors within the market.

1.2. User Research & Persona creation

The second part involved user research aimed at identifying potential users and gathering deeper insights into their needs and preferences. Subsequently, we crafted personas to represent distinct segments of our target audience based on these findings.

2.1. Wireframing & Clickdummy

Following the user research phase, we transitioned into the creation and ideation stage. Here, we developed wireframes from scratch, engaging in numerous ideation sessions to refine our concepts. Upon reaching a satisfactory outcome, we proceeded to create a prototype and clickable dummy to further visualize and refine our ideas.

2.2. Preparation of the UX Testing

Next, we embarked on preparing for UX testing by formulating scenarios and tasks aimed at gathering insights into the usability and effectiveness of the current version.

3. UX Testing Evaluation

Following the UX testing sessions, we proceeded to evaluate the gathered results comprehensively. Based on these findings, we formulated actionable insights and identified key areas for improvement, outlining the next steps required to enhance the product.
