The sustainable fintess app

The project's objective was to create an application empowering users to lower their daily CO2 emissions. The sustainable Fitness App was called "Sustaina-Fit." This app allows users to explore local supermarkets for recipes aligned with their fitness objectives while promoting the purchase of locally sourced products, thereby minimizing CO2 emissions.

Timeline

22.12.2022 - 22.03.2023

Skills

App Design / Mobile / Brand Design / Wireframing / Prototyping / User Research / Design Thinking

1. Phase - Design Thinking & Wireframing

In the initial month, we embarked on the Design Thinking and ideation phase, seeking to uncover the app's purpose and concept. During this time, we opted to develop a Fitness App named "Sustaina-Fit." We formulated the initial concept and proceeded to craft the initial wireframes.

2. Phase - Brand Design

The focal point of the second phase revolved around establishing a Brand Identity and its encompassing elements such as color utilization, shadows, corners, typography, styles, and components. This stage demanded extensive ideation and research to achieve a final design that embraced simplicity and clarity. Our objective was to incorporate ample white space and utilize accent colors to create a cohesive visual experience.

3. Phase - App Design & Clickdummy

The final phase encompassed the App's actual construction, encompassing the transition from low-fi Wireframes to finished designs and the creation of the Clickdummy. During this stage, we gained valuable insights into the intricacies of detailed prototyping and its associated elements. The key challenge was ensuring a seamless finish, given the diverse range of screens, and establishing the necessary connections between them.
