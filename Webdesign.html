The sustainable Bioladen Körnig

The objective of the project was to develop a design for an eco-friendly web shop, enabling users to order food packages or individual items as a means to minimize daily CO2 emissions and embrace a more sustainable lifestyle. The products featured in the shop are locally sourced as well.

Timeline

05.07.2022 - 05.10.2022

Skills

Webdesign / Branddesign / Wireframing / Protoyping / Research

1. Phase - Concept

The first phase was about developing a sustainable concept for the Bioladen Körnig. Through a first visual board the company should see how their website could look like. In order to do so we created a first impression board with key elements like the Logo, the colors and potential card designs.

2. Phase - Brand Design

The second phase was the development of a brand design / identity that would suit and be ideal for the website. There the main focus was on the used elements, colors and typography.

3. Phase / Part 1 - Component Design

For the first part of the third phase we created components that were essential for the prototype and later the Webdesign. Caroussels and other card styles needed to be defined, tested and created.

3. Phase / Part 2 - Design & Prototyping

The second part of the third phase was the final realization of the design and a functioning prototype to be further used by the developers in the future in order to create the website.
